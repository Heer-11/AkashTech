About Us:


June 04, 2020Posted by Heer Shastri

 

For over 40 years, Navratna Group has been synonymous with unparalleled properties in Gujarat. With a passion spanning four decades, the group’s excellence in building Homes, Home Sites, Lifestyle Communities, Work & Retail Spaces, has created a covetable sphere of influence & admiration in Gujarat’s real estate industry.

Each project executed by the Navratna Group bears a mark of distinction. With state-of-the-art design, sustainable architecture and cutting- edge technology, the properties developed under the umbrella of Navratna developers have contributed in shaping the real estate landscape of Gujarat. The bespoke brand plunged into the business of construction, initially by engaging itself into residential development projects, followed by construction of some of the iconic commercial business buildings in Ahmedabad. The company rapidly grew by leaps and bounds to change the urban landscape by incorporating green architecture and green spaces into their lifestyle community projects. Leveraging their real estate and construction expertise as a visionary developer, Navratna has constructed buildings that have helped businesses achieve the optimum value in functionality, location and cost. Today, Navratna stands as a trail blazer with aggressive growth plans spread across the lifestyle communities, retail and commercial sectors, and desires to be a top notch group that promises growth of the investors, associates and everyone who has been a part of the Navratna Family!!
